How To Use
Paint Calc is designed to calculate the amount of paint required for painting walls. It will deduct any window or door measurements you input.

  1. Input Width and Height measurement for each wall.
  2. Input window or door measurements to deduct.
  3. Click calculate.
 All measurements should be in metres. Paint coverage information can be obtained from the manufacturer or the paint container. This is an estimate. Please confirm quantities with your supplier before purchasing.
